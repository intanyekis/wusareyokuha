Who is Leigh-Allyn Baker?

Leigh-Allyn Baker is an American actress and voice actress who is famous for her role in ‘Will & Grace’ in 1998, ‘Good Luck Charlie’ in 2010, and ‘The 7D’ in 2014.

Leigh-Allyn Baker is also popular for her voice role of Abby on the Nickelodeon animated series ‘Back at the Barnyard’.

Leigh-Allyn Baker: Age, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

She was born on March 13, 1972, in Murray, Kentucky, USA. She is currently 50 years old. Her father’s name is Vicki and her mother’s name is Mike Baker.

Leigh-Allyn Baker has got a brother named Chuck Baker and he is the owner and CEO of Kentucky Lake Oil Company.

Baker holds American citizenship but her ethnicity is unknown.

Leigh-Allyn Baker: Professional Life and Career

Leigh-Allyn Baker debuted her film career with the role of Mitzi in ‘Shrunken Heads in 1994. After a year, she appeared as a Waitress in ‘Leprechaun 2’. Then, she appeared as Implant Removal Patient in ‘Breast Man’ in 1997, Mary in ‘Very Mean Man’ in 2000, Liz Morgan in ‘Bad Hair Day’ in 2015, Leah Singer in ‘Swiped’ in 2017, etc.

In addition, she guest-starred in the television shows such as ‘That ‘70s Show’ ‘Early Edition’, and also starred in the short0lived 1996 sitcom ‘The Last Frontier’.

Leigh-Allyn Baker also portrayed the recurring role of Hannah Webster in the first season of ‘Charmed’. Also. She appeared in a recurring role, Ellen in ‘Will & Grace’ from 1998 to 2006.

Leigh-Allyn Baker is also known for providing the voice of Abby in the Nickelodeon animated series ‘Back at the Barnyard’. She made her appearance as Mickey in ‘Hannah Montana’ from 2008 to 2009. She was in the late weeks of pregnancy with her son Griffin during the completion of the 3rd season of ‘Hannah Montana’.

Then, Leigh-Allyn Baker appeared as Amy Duncan in ‘Good Luck Charlie’ from 2010 to 2014 and recently appeared as herself in the ‘Battle of the Network Stars’ in 2017.

Awards, Nominations

Leigh-Allyn Baker won BTVA Television Voice Acting Award in the category Best Vocal Ensemble in a Television Series for ‘The 7D’ in 2016, won BTVA Television Voice Acting Award in the category Best Vocal Ensemble in a New Television Series for ‘The 7D’ in 2015, and won TV Guide Award in the category Editor’s Choice in ‘Will & Grace’ in 2006.

Who is Renni Rucci?

Renni Rucci is renowned as an American hip-hop rapper, Model, and popular Instagram personality.

She rose to fame through her singles such as ‘Roll In Peace’, ‘Everybody”, Bank Account’, and so on.

The rapper is professionally known as Renni Rucci but her real name is Courtney Rene.

Who are the parents of Renni Rucci?

Renni Rucci was born on October 2, 1991, in Irmo, South Carolina. She holds an American nationality and belongs of mixed descent Irish and African-American.

She hasn’t disclosed her parent’s and sibling’s information to the public. However, her father is of Irish descent whereas her mother is of African-American descent.

Renni Rucci: Professional Life, Career

Renni Rucci started her career as an exotic dancer. At the same time, she is actively engaging on social media sites from where she built her fanbase and started her modeling career.

She signed with a prestigious modeling agency based in Colombia and worked for several brands. She has also appeared in numerous popular magazines.

In 2017, Renni Rucci began her music career, and her first songs that gained huge attention were remixes of “Bodak Yellow” and Bank Account” released in August.

Then, she released her single, Roll in Peace in December 2017 under Atlantic Records.

In October 2018, she collaborated with Skully and released the hot single titled “Asum” produced by MemphisMoneyBags.

She dropped another single “Cold Heartes” ft Lil Yachty in the same month.

Renni Rucci latest music video is “Can’t Be” which was released on March 21, 2021, on her self-titled YouTube channel. The video has garnered over 1.3M views to date.

Similarly, she released “Hands-on Ya Knees” on January 11, 2021 feat with Kevin Gates.

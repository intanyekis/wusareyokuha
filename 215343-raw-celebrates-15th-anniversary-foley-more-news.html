RAW Celebrates 15th Anniversary, Foley, More News

Publish date: 2024-08-14


Source – PWInsider

WWE is currently gearing up to promote the 15th anniversary of Monday Night RAW that is coming up this January. A special DVD set will be released to commemorate the milestone.

The final RAW taping of the year will be held on Saturday, December 29th in Greensboro, North Carolina with an airdate of December 31st. The final SmackDown/ECW show will be taped on December 30th in Richmond, Virginia.

Mick Foley will be making a special appearance at Spookyworld in Boston, MA next Saturday, October 29th. Depending on how the voting goes, we could see Foley as a guest referee at Cyber Sunday.

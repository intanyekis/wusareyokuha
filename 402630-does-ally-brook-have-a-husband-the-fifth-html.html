Brooke was born in 1993 in San Antonio. The 29-year-old, best known for her seductive and sexy performances while on stage, once shocked fans when she announced she was a virgin and intended to stay that way until marriage.

The singer, who made headlines after revealing sensitive information about her private life, told Page Six that even though she was a virgin, she had encountered several temptations. Ally Brooke Is Very 'Proud' of Her Virginity

When Brooke opened up about her being a virgin in her book, "Finding Your Harmony," the singer said she was brave enough to share the information because it was something she held dear to her heart. In a Zoom interview, she said she was "proud of her personal decision" and added:

"I’m happy I can share it with my fans and be honest, but it's also awesome because I can still be flirtatious and be sexy and fun in my music videos and in my songs."

She, however, admitted to TMZ that it was a tough road for a person of her age and status to reveal that she hadn't had sex. Her revelation sparked a lot of reactions from fans wondering why the "Worth It" singer was still a virgin even though she was beautiful, successful, and famous.

According to the Texas native, she is a virgin by choice and is saving herself for marriage. As a beautiful young woman, Brooke admitted that there were moments when she encountered temptations, and when that happened, she stopped the desire by killing the mood.

While Ally Brooke's dating life remains a mystery, the singer has been romantically linked to her manager Will Bracey and Troy Ogletree in the past.

Some of the celebrities she got inspiration from who were not in a hurry to lose their virginity include Jordin Sparks and singer, actress, and entrepreneur Jessica Simpson, who was previously married to Nick Lachey. Brooke then encouraged other virgins to stick to their commitment and wait until marriage.

She then reassured others to stay strong and not to feel alone, insisting that many people were also going through the same struggles and challenges. "I know it's tough, but you really can do it," she said.

Is Ally Brooke Married Now?

Camilla Belle News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Name:Camilla Belle

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Birthday:October 2, 1986

About

Camilla Belle was born on October 2, 1986 in L.A.. She made her professional debut in a print ad at the age of nine months. Commercials soon followed and by the time she was five, she’d landed her first TV movie role in Troubleshooters: Trapped Beneath the Earth.

She has starred in the thriller, When a Stranger Calls, the prehistoric epic, 10,000 BC, and Push, a sci-fi drama.

Belle dated teen heartthrob Joe Jonas from 2008-2009.

Biography

Kristine Hermosa was born in Quezon City, Philippines on September 9, 1983. One of the most popular actresses in the Philippines, she stars as Yna Macaspac in the primetime drama series Pangako Sa ‘Yo. She co-stars with Jericho Rosales on Pangako Sa ‘Yo. She was discovered at 12, accompanying her older sister to an audition for the network ABS-CBN; she was given her first part in the 1998 drama series Sa Sandaling Kailangan Mo Ako.

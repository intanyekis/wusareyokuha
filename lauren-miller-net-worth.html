Lauren Anne Miller Rogen (born October 30, 1982) is an American actress and screenwriter who has appeared in the films Superbad (2007), Zack and Miri Make a Porno (2008), and 50/50 (2011). In 2012, she starred in the film For a Good Time, Call..., which she also co-wrote and produced. Her husband is actor Seth Rogen. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿

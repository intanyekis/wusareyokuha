TM88 Confirms Collabo Mixtape With Wiz Khalifa &amp; Juicy J

Publish date: 2024-06-15

Yesterday (Mar. 21), Juicy J dropped a bomb on his upcoming project with Wiz Khalifa. The Three 6 Mafia founder posted the tracklist for TGODMAFIA, but was quick to remove it.

Before anyone could question the mixtape’s existence, 808 Mafia’s TM88 re-posted the list on Instagram with the caption “88 x Juicy J x Wiz.”

With the tracklist floating all over the Internet, Juicy J took to Snpachat to give fans a preview of the tape. He also hinted that the project could drop at any time this week. “Me, Wiz, TM88. Should I drop this project tonite [sic]? Or get high and drop it later?” he tweeted.

Me Wiz TM88 should I drop this project tonite ? Or get high & drop it later ?

TM88, who constructed memorable hits like Drake’s “Company” and French Montana’s “Moses,” serves as the executive producer of the 14-track mixtape. The only feature to appear on the tracklist is One Direction’s Liam Payne, who previewed his collaboration with Juicy J called “You.”

Snippet of Liam singing on Juicy J’s “You,” which will be on his new mixtape pic.twitter.com/qSihuB6Zxp

— The Daily Payne (@The_Daily_Payne) March 21, 2016

The last time the Atlanta super producer, Juicy J and Wiz linked up was for “Respect” with K. Camp off Cabin Fever 3. TGODMAFIA comes a couple months after TM88’s plans to drop his highly-anticipated joint mixtape with PARTYNEXTDOOR Party At 8 fell through.

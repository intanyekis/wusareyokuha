Gordon Ramsay Opening Upscale Burger Restaurant In Chicago

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Celebrity Chef Gordom Ramsay has some exciting news for fans in Chicago. They're about to be graced with the opening of his hot new restaurant, and this will be one of many more to come. Ramsay took to Instagram to reveal the exciting news that he plans to open an upscale burger restaurant in River North, and fans are already salivating.

As the excitement continues to build, TimeOut reports that this restaurant not only has a famous, celebrity chef backing it, but it will also be one of the most ritzy burger joints the city has ever seen. Ramsay has secured a 5,000 square foot property that will be situated at the corner of Ontario and State, and their doors will open soon.

There are lots of other burger restaurants in Chicago, but none of them are quite like this one! Staying true to his brand and carrying the high standards of his name, Chef Ramsay is pulling all the stops to make this restaurant a cut above the rest. This hot new location will come equipped with an island bar and will seat a whopping 120 diners.

Ramsay is a real fan of the Chicago restaurant scene, and he wanted to bring the best burgers to the city... so he did. Ramsay currently owns Flame Burger in Las Vegas, but this new spot is going to have a much higher end, upscale vibe.

According to TimeOut, many aspects of this restaurant will offer an elevated experience.  They go on to say that the Michelin-starred chef has teased the new location by describing his; "delicious, butter-based burgers, which blend different cuts of beef meant to impart a bolder flavor. Specialty items include the Hell’s Kitchen Burger, a signature patty dressed up with with asadero cheese, roasted jalapeños, avocado, roasted tomatoes and house-made jalapeño aioli."

Of course, there will be other items on the menu aside from burgers. Ramsay's menu will also included fries, milkshakes, and a variety of other fast food options that are designed with a unique, upscale twist.

Fans of Ramsay already know that everything he touches turns to gold, and the expectations for his new burger restaurant are soaring high. Aside from the distinctly different ambiance of an upscale burger place, fans are already salivating, knowing that Ramsay's culinary skills will surely result in some of the tastiest burgers Chicago has ever experienced.

Gordan Ramsay Burger is slated to open at the end of 2021, or early 2022 at the latest.

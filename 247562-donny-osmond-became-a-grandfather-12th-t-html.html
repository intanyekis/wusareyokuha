Donny Osmond Became a Grandfather for the 12th Time Meet His Newborn Grandson Christian

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Donny Osmond of “Donny & Marie” recently became a grandfather for the 12th time. The doting patriarch shared the great news via Instagram, introducing his newborn grandson to his followers.

Singer Donny Osmond is a grandfather for the 12th time. The excited patriarch took to Instagram to share the great news with heartwarming photos of the newest addition to his family. He wrote in the caption:

“We officially have a dozen! Debbie and I are so excited that our 12th grandbaby was born two weeks ago.”

Donny Osmond poses at "Dancing with the Stars" Season 27 at CBS Television City on October 2, 2018 | Photo: Getty Images

Osmond wrote that his eldest son Donny Jr., and his wife Jessi blessed him with a new grandchild. He noted that they welcomed their fourth son named Christian on Tuesday, December 29, and the whole family is in love with him already.

The first slide was a black-and-white photo of baby Christian wrapped in a white wool blanket and sweetly asleep. It was followed by an adorable snapshot of the new parents-of-four doting on their little one.

In the comments section, congratulations rang in for the former teen idol from his more than 200,000 followers. Many described Christian’s birth as a wonderful way to start the new year for the family.

In an August social media post, the seasoned entertainer revealed the sweet nickname his grandchildren call him.

In November, the “Scared Emotion” crooner became a grandfather for the 11th time when his son Chris welcomed a daughter, Aussie Rae, with his wife Alta. Osmond shared the news on Thanksgiving via Instagram.

He blessed fans with heart-melting photos of the baby girl, including a sweet image of Chris and Alta falling in love with their daughter in the hospital room. The “Donny & Marie” star described Thanksgiving as "extra special," thanks to Aussie.

The 63-year-old is a proud father of five sons who have given him 12 wonderful grandchildren he is totally in love with. He never fails to show that they are a priority to him and take time to spend with the youngest members of the brood.

In the caption, Osmond revealed that his grandchildren call him “Bapa.” Since ending his 11-year Las Vegas residency with his sister, Marie Osmond, he has been focused on connecting with family, and his social media updates are proof of it.

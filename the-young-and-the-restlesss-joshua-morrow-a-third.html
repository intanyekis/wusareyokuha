The Young and the Restlesss Joshua Morrow a Third-Time Dad!

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Soaps.com is delighted to report that The Young and the Restless’s Joshua Morrow (Nick Newman), and his beautiful wife Tobe, have welcomed into the world yet another gorgeous little bundle of joy! Cash Joshua Morrow came into the world on April 21st and weighed 6lbs 15oz!

Joshua and Tobe’s new addition will join older siblings Cooper and Crew in the family fold. Joshua told the media that Tobe is amazing and beautiful as ever, and that Cash is perfect. He also reported that it was a very drama-free birth experience!

Congratulations Joshua, Tobe, Cooper, and Crew from Soaps.com and Y&R viewers! Enjoy your new family member!

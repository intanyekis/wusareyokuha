Park Shin Hye and Park Hyung Sik Battle for Academic Supremacy in 'Doctor Slump'

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Park Hyung Shik and Park Shin Hye are getting ready to make a triumphant return with “Doctor Slump”! The romantic comedy “Doctor Slump” follows Park Hyung Sik’s Yeo Jung Woo and Park Shin Hye’s Nam Ha Neul as they attempt to revive their lives after falling into a slump after only ever following successful paths. In the midst of their deepest despair, the two bitter enemies will find solace in one other’s company and become each other’s beacons of hope, bringing joy, excitement, and comfort to those around them.

For the first time in a drama in a decade, following their successful collaboration on “Heirs,” Park Shin Hye and Park Hyung Sik reunite in “Doctor Slump.” Writer Baek Sun Woo of “What’s Wrong With Secretary Kim” and director Oh Hyun Jong of “Find Me in Your Memory” and “Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok Joo” will collaborate on the drama.

Park Hyung Sik and Park Shin Hye’s captivating chemistry is showcased in the recently released teaser poster, which harkens back to the days when Nam Ha Neul and Yeo Jung Woo initially crossed paths at Youngwon High School.

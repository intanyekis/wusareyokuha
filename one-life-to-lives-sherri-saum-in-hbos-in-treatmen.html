One Life to Lives Sherri Saum In HBOs In Treatment!

Publish date: 2024-07-20

It’s been a long time since we last saw RJ Gannon’s (Timothy D. Stickney) daughter Keri Reynolds, but the former One Life to Live actress, Sherri Saum, has just landed a new gig!

Sherri will play a psychiatric patient in the HBO drama “In Treatment,” which begins its second season in 2009!

Since her character met her fate after jumping from a balcony to her death in 2003, Sherri has appeared in a handful of films and Primetime hits, and even snagged herself a sexy Latino husband on May 19, 2007 – our very own Kamar de los Reyes (Antonio Vega)!

Soaps.com wants to congratulate Sherri on her new role!

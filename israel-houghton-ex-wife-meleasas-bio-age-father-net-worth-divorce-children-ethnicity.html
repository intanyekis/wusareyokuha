Who is Meleasa Houghton?

Meleasa Houghton, born on the 1st of March, 1964, is an American gospel singer who became famous through being a member of the band Israel and New Breed. She is also the ex-wife of well-known gospel singer Israel Houghton.

Meleasa Houghton’s Career

In terms of her career, she met her now ex-husband, Israel Houghton in a church ministry in Pittsburgh. Being a singer herself, she eventually became part of Israel’s band, Israel and New Breed. The band’s first album was released in 2002 entitled “Real.” It was then followed by another album entitled “Live From Another Level” in 2004, as well as “Alive in South Africa” in 2005. And “Live a Deeper Level” in 2007.

In 2009, Israel and New Breed released another album called “A Timeless Christmas”, followed by “New Season Life” in 2011. The following year, they released a compilation of their greatest hits in the album “The Best of Israel Houghton & New Breed from 2002-2012.” Other compilation albums that they released include “Jesus At the Center” released in 2012, and “Playlist: The Very Best of Israel & New Breed” released in 2015. The success of Israel & New Breed helped tremendously in increasing Houghton’s net worth.

Aside from lending her vocals to the band Israel and New Breed, Houghton is also known to have contributed to some of the bands greatest hits such as “ Rez Power”, “Our Presence is Heaven”, and “Jesus at the Center.” She also made contributions to the songs “Alpha and Omega”, “Chasing Me Down”, “How Awesome Is Our God” and “It’s Not Over” among a few other hits.

Houghton’s work with Israel and New Breed also led to several accolades, including a GMA Dove Award for Traditional Gospel Record Song of the Year in 2015, and a nomination for the Grammy Award for Best Gospel Performance/Song.

Israel and New Breed’s last album was released in 2015 entitled “Covered: Alive in Asia.” Since then, Houghton has been on hiatus in her career.

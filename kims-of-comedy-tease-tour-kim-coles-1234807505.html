&quot;The Kims Of Comedy&quot; Tease Tour Following &quot;Epic Night&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-15

After a successful “The Kims Of Comedy” event, it looks like Kim Fields, Kim Wayans, Kym Whitley, and Kim Coles might have more plans in the works.

The experience took place last week (Oct. 27) in Wilmington, Del., at the city’s public library. According to the official description, the all-ages event was free for attendees to sit in on a conversation between the comedic talents about their individual lives and careers in the entertainment industry and more. No registration or tickets were required with seating provided on a first come, first served basis.

Kim Fields served as the host, and offered a book signing for her work Wisdom G.I.F.T.S: Finding and Embracing The Legacy In Our Lessons after the discussion concluded.

Following the roundtable, Coles shared a photo of the group on Instagram, teasing that the library appearance may be the first of more events in various cities.

“This is THE moment that no one gets to see,” began the caption. “When performers are backstage getting ready to share, their light, wisdom, and joy with an audience. These backstage moments are often not glamorous or fancy but are the times when we get to connect and reflect before being with all the beautiful people out there!”

She continued after thanking the Wilmington crowd, “We shared. We laughed. We learned from each other and it was an epic night! I think we need to put the show on the road!!! What city do you think we should bring it to next?”

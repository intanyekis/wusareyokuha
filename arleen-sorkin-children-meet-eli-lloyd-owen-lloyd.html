Arleen Sorkin children: Meet Eli Lloyd &amp; Owen Lloyd

Publish date: 2024-06-11

The famous American actress, comedian, presenter, and playwright Arleen Sorkin was born on October 14, 1955. She became well-known for her role as Calliope Jones in the NBC daytime drama “Days of Our Lives.”

However, she is most known for playing Harley Quinn, a renowned DC Comics villain, in the landmark animated series “Batman: The Animated Series” and its later adaptations in other animated programs and computer games. From her birth on October 14, 1955, to her death on August 26, 2023, Arleen Sorkin lived a 67-year life.
She began her acting career by portraying the lovable Calliope Jones on “Days of Our Lives” from 1984 until 1990, with follow-up appearances in later years.

Arleen Sorkin children: Meet Eli Lloyd &amp; Owen Lloyd

Eli and Owen, who were born in 1997 and 2000, respectively, had the good fortune to have parents who were both extraordinarily gifted. Christopher and Arleen frequently placed a high value on family time in order to provide their children a well-rounded childhood.

Arleen began her career in cabaret in the late 1970s before moving to television, where she made an enduring impression in parts on “Days of Our Lives” and “Batman: The Animated Series”.
